# v1.17.0 Program Investment Bonuses

Date: 2026-06-18
Release type: Minor

## Decision

Make Program Investment points immediately affect the active dynasty systems they are meant to improve.

## Why Minor

This changes live recruiting budget accounting and offseason player-development outcomes, so it is gameplay balance rather than a narrow UI fix.

## Scope

- `investProgramPoint` now reconciles the active recruiting budget after investment changes.
- Sunk recruiting points remain spent when the budget changes.
- Training and facilities now have a stronger infrastructure role in preseason development.
- Added tests for immediate recruiting budget refresh and high-vs-low infrastructure development output.

## Expert Audit

The program-investment audit found that `programPoints` changed team ratings but did not refresh active `seasonBudget`, `weeklyPoints`, or `pointsRemaining`. It also confirmed training/facilities were present in development math but too diluted to reliably change outcomes.
