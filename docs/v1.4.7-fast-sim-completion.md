# v1.4.7 Fast Sim Completion

## Decision

`simulateSeasons` now counts completed season-history entries and completes the final offseason when the target reaches the dynasty maximum.

## Why

The Debug Sim To End control requested 20 simulated seasons, but the helper stopped at Year 20 kickoff because it targeted the next regular-season phase rather than completed seasons. That left the dynasty short of its intended `complete` phase.

## Scope

- Reworked `simulateSeasons` to target completed season history.
- Preserved the existing shorter fast-sim behavior that returns at the next regular-season kickoff.
- Added regression coverage for a full 20-year sim producing 20 history entries and `phase: "complete"`.

- `npm run test -- src/sim/__tests__/dynasty.test.ts`
