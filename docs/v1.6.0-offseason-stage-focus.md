# v1.6.0 Offseason Stage Focus

## Decision

The Overview offseason report now renders one focused stage at a time: departures, recruiting, signing day, or development.

## Why

The offseason dashboard previously showed departures, classes, walk-ons, recruiting rankings, development, and program review together. That made the page hard to scan and mixed future-stage content into the current task.

## Scope

- Added a shared offseason stage helper.
- Limited departure, recruiting, signing, and development panels to their active stage.
- Added a compact recruiting focus panel for late offseason recruiting weeks.
- Updated smoke assertions and screenshots for staged offseason behavior.

## Validation

- `npm run smoke`
