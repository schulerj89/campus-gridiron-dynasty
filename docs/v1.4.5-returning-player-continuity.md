# v1.4.5 Returning Player Continuity

## Decision

Signing day no longer trims the combined roster before preseason development applies the offseason departure report.

## Why

The offseason report is generated when the season ends, but signing day previously trimmed each roster immediately after adding signees. That meant returning players who were not listed as graduates or pro departures could disappear before preseason development.

## Scope

- Removed pre-departure roster trimming from signing day.
- Kept departures as the source of truth for returning-player removal.
- Added a seed-based regression that verifies all non-departing players from a team survive into preseason.
