# v1.20.2 Recruiting Full-Board Pitch Guard

## Expert Finding

The code-review expert found that `pitchRecruit` could spend points on an offered prospect who was not on the user's board while the board was already full. Offer and scout actions already guarded against that case, but pitch still called the board-add helper after spending points.

## Decision

Treat this as a patch-level recruiting integrity fix. The action should be blocked before any RNG, point spend, investment accounting, interest movement, or board mutation happens.

## Fix

- Added a board-limit guard to `pitchRecruit`.
- Reused that limit when writing the board after a valid pitch.
- Updated the recruiting card and modal pitch buttons to disable when an off-board recruit is blocked by a full board.
- Added a regression test for an offered off-board recruit with exactly enough pitch points and a full board.
