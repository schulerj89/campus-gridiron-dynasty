# v1.4.2 Committed Recruit Stage Integrity Decision

## Code-Review Expert Finding

Weekly recruiting advancement sent already-committed recruits through top-school narrowing. At later weeks, that could overwrite `stage: "softPledge"` with `top5` or `top3` while `committedTeamId` remained set.

## Patch Decision

Skip weekly school movement and top-school narrowing for recruits that already have `committedTeamId`, matching the existing signed-recruit skip.

## Why

- A commitment should stay a commitment until signing day or later explicit flip logic.
- Board refund logic can still detect the committed recruit and free/refund board investment.
- Keeping stage and committed team aligned makes UI labels and save state easier to trust.

## Validation

- Added a Week 7 regression proving a committed board recruit remains `softPledge`, board investment is refunded, and signed recruits stay signed.
- Refreshed smoke screenshots with the visible `v1.4.2` badge.
