# v1.21.16 Potential-Capped Development

## Release Type

Patch release. This is a player attribute logic fix.

## Code-Review/Stats Expert Decision

The code-review/stats expert found that players at their potential could still gain attributes during preseason development because the growth budget allowed extra room even when the potential gap was zero.

## Why

Overall was capped, but unit ratings read attributes directly. That meant capped players could secretly improve team passing, blocking, coverage, or other units without visible OVR growth.

## Changes

- Set preseason development growth budget to zero when `overall >= potential`.
- Preserved normal growth and breakout behavior for players with real potential room.
- Added a regression that verifies at-potential players keep the same attributes and produce no progression row.
