# v1.4.8 Full Board Scouting

## Decision

Scouting an off-board prospect now requires room on the recruiting board before any points are spent.

## Why

`scoutRecruit` previously spent points and recorded recruit investment before attempting to add the recruit to the board. If the board was full, the add failed silently and left sunk points tied to a recruit outside the normal board commitment/refund path.

## Scope

- Made scout spending atomic with board insertion.
- Disabled Scout in the recruit card and detail modal for off-board prospects when the board is full.
- Added regression coverage for full-board scout attempts and normal off-board scouting when space remains.

## Validation

- `npm run test -- src/sim/__tests__/recruiting.test.ts`
