# v1.20.20 Scoped Honor Awards

## Expert Finding

The code-review expert found that honor teams persisted position labels such as `Receiver` or `Quarterback` as award names. That made player award history and record-book counts ambiguous because All-American and all-conference honors could share the same raw label.

## Decision

Treat this as a patch-level logic fix. It changes only persisted/display labels for award history while leaving Awards page card layout, season award generation, and selection logic unchanged.

## Fix

- Added scoped season-award label helpers for player and team history.
- Kept national trophy names unchanged.
- Prefixed honor-team labels with their scope, such as `All-American First Team - Receiver` and `{Conference} First Team - Receiver`.
- Added a regression test that follows an All-American label into player awards and archived team history.
