# v1.11.4 Patch: Generation Name Pools

## Decision

Expand the fictional city, first-name, and last-name pools, and draw team cities without replacement during world generation.

## Why

The previous city pool had only 26 entries for 70 teams, so duplicate team cities were common. Player and recruit names also reused a small first/last combination space across thousands of generated people.

## Verification

- Added generation coverage for non-repeating team cities.
- Added generation coverage for improved player and recruit name diversity.
