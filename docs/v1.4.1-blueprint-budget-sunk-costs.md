# v1.4.1 Blueprint Budget Sunk-Cost Decision

## Code-Review Expert Finding

`refreshRecruitingBudget` recomputed `pointsSpent` from the new budget and remaining points. When a player had already spent recruiting points and then rebuilt the Program Blueprint into a lower recruiting-budget allocation, the recalculation could erase sunk spend.

## Patch Decision

Treat `pointsSpent` as the source of truth during Blueprint budget refreshes. Clamp spent points to the new season budget, then derive remaining points as `seasonBudget - pointsSpent`.

## Why

- Recruiting actions are intended to be season-long sunk costs.
- Blueprint rebuilds should change future budget capacity, not refund past scouting or offer decisions.
- Keeping the invariant in one refresh helper protects both manual allocation and auto-build flows.

## Validation

- Added a regression that spends points under a max recruiting-reach blueprint, rebuilds to a lower recruiting-budget allocation, and verifies spent points remain sunk.
- Refreshed smoke screenshots with the visible `v1.4.1` badge.
