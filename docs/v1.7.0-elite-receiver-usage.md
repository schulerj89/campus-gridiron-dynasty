# v1.7.0 Elite Receiver Usage

## Decision

Receiving yards and receiving touchdowns now use role-aware target weights so WR1 and elite WRs receive a larger share of existing team passing production.

## Why

The stat expert found that team passing volume was in range, but receiving distribution was too flat across 5-7 targets. A 90+ overall WR could land near 17-22% of team receiving yards, making 1,000-yard seasons unlikely even in strong passing offenses.

## Scope

- Kept target participation and team passing volume unchanged.
- Added receiving skill weights with WR1/WR2/WR3 role multipliers and a small elite-WR bonus.
- Used the same receiving weights for yards and touchdown splits.
- Added game-sim coverage for elite receiver share, target participation, and 12-game season production.
