# v1.21.10 Preseason Roster Integrity

## Decisions

- Logic fix: high-star recruit floors now require four-stars to start at 68+ overall and five-stars at 74+ overall. The previous trait-band math could create 57-58 overall four-stars and 69 overall five-stars, which broke user expectations for recruiting quality.
- Bug fix: preseason now enforces a 105-player roster limit after walk-ons are added. Cuts are selected from the lowest overall and potential players while preserving each position's `TARGET_ROSTER` minimum.
- Small refactor: roster cutdown results are stored on `TeamOffseasonReport` and manual depth-chart order is pruned after cuts. This keeps the UI, saves, and simulation usage from referencing removed players.
- UI fix: preseason development now focuses on returning players sorted by largest overall gain, incoming freshmen are filtered out, departures sort by highest overall first, national players of the week show only offensive and defensive winners, and season award candidate rank badges keep their numbers centered.
