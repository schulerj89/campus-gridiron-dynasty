# v1.4.3 Matchup Top-25 Rank Decision

## Code-Review Expert Finding

Matchup previews read ranks from `rankings[0].allEntries`, so every team could appear with a rank such as `#45`. That conflicted with the app's Top 25 poll language and made ordinary games look like ranked matchups.

## Patch Decision

Read matchup ranks from `rankings[0].entries`, the Top 25 poll list, instead of the full 1-70 ranking table.

## Why

- The app already keeps full 1-70 ranks for the Rankings page and movement history.
- Matchup stakes should reserve ranked labels for Top 25 teams.
- This keeps dashboard and schedule preview language consistent with the poll UI.

## Validation

- Added a regression that leaves user/opponent full-poll ranks outside the Top 25 and verifies the matchup preview treats both as unranked.
- Refreshed smoke screenshots with the visible `v1.4.3` badge.
