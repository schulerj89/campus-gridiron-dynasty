# v1.3.6 Legacy Relationship Sanitization Decision

## Code-Review Expert Finding

`normalizeDynastyState` filled missing legacy fields but trusted relationship IDs from old saves. A stale `userTeamId`, recruit offer, top-school, interest, commitment, board, or investment ID could point at a missing team or inactive recruit before the app rendered or advanced.

## Patch Decision

Normalize relationship IDs during save loading. The migration now falls back to a valid user team, filters recruit school references, clears invalid commitments, rebuilds empty recruit interest from a valid team, and removes signed, committed, duplicate, or missing recruits from the active board and investment map.

## Why

- Recruiting simulation assumes team and recruit IDs are valid once state is loaded.
- Legacy saves should not assign players to nonexistent teams or keep stale committed recruits on the user's board.
- The fix keeps corruption handling local to storage migration instead of spreading defensive checks through the simulation.

## Validation

- Added a corrupted-save regression for stale user-team IDs, invalid recruit school references, inactive board recruits, and stale investment records.
- Smoke refreshes core screenshots with the visible patch version.
