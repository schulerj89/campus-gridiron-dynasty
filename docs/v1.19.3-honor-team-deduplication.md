# v1.19.3 Honor Team Deduplication

## Decision

Treat this as a patch release because it fixes award integrity for existing All-American and all-conference honor teams.

## Why

First and second honor teams were selected independently. Duplicate position slots, especially WR and OL, could cause a first-team player to appear on the second team too.

## Implementation

- First teams are selected first.
- Second teams are selected from the same pool while excluding first-team player IDs.
- Freshman teams remain independent because they are a separate honor scope.
